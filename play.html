How to play

What the game is

This game is a grid based board game.
The players can move the circle shaped icons.
However, the player can only control the icon with their color.
When the circle icon moves, it will leave a cross icon with the same color as the circle.
This icon blocks the movement of both circles.
The player can move their own cross icons around
The circle icon which gets to the other side first wins.
The player who does each turn first is calculated with the Thue Morse sequence

How to play

In each turn the player can move the circle icon, and move a cross icon once.
The color behind the buttons on the side indicates whose turn it is.
When the turn is a move turn, the player can press one of the nine buttons to move the circle in the direction of that button.
When the turn is an edit turn, the player can click squares to select them, and click them again to deselect them.

Selection patterns

The player can select two of their own cross icons, and one of the opponents cross icons to remove their cross icon at the expense of two of your own.
The player can select two of their cross icons and one empty square to create a cross icon there at the expense of two others.
If a selection follows neither of those, nothing will happen.
When a player is happy with a selection, they can press any of the buttons on the side to apply those rules, and do the next turn.
